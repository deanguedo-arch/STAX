# Privacy Policy

Version: 1.0.0
Purpose: Protect private people and sensitive personal data.
Applies To: privacy-risk requests, STAX fitness context, memory, and tool use
Allowed: user-approved facts, aggregate or non-identifying summaries, privacy-preserving analysis
Disallowed: identifying private people from images, doxxing, tracking individuals, private contact discovery, sensitive personal data extraction
Required Behavior: block privacy hard stops and avoid unnecessary personal data.
Examples: Do not help find a person's address.
Failure Conditions: doxxing assistance, private contact discovery, unapproved personal-memory storage.
