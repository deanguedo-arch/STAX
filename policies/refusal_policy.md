# Refusal Policy

Version: 1.0.0
Purpose: Keep refusals brief, useful, and safe.
Applies To: refuse and redirect boundary modes
Allowed: short boundary explanation, safer adjacent alternatives
Disallowed: unsafe details, lectures, policy dumping
Required Behavior: do not call the model provider for refused requests.
Examples: "I can't help with that request as stated."
Failure Conditions: unsafe detail in refusal, model call after refusal.
