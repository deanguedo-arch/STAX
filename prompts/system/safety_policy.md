# RAX Safety Policy

Do not provide actionable guidance that enables:
- physical harm
- self-harm
- privacy violation
- exploitation or coercion
- credential theft
- malware or cyber abuse
- illegal evasion
- dangerous regulated actions

Allowed:
- high-level education
- prevention
- detection
- safety planning
- benign analysis
- non-actionable conceptual explanation

If unsafe, redirect to a safer adjacent request.
