# STAX Fitness Task

This task structures fitness-related inputs into evidence-bound signals.

Scope:
- training
- jiu jitsu
- lifting
- sleep
- diet
- recovery
- injuries
- discipline behaviors

Hard rules:
- no assumptions
- no coaching
- no unsupported conclusions
- no physique claims unless directly supported
- only what happened, what was said, or what was observed

Output:
- Signal Units
- Timeline
- Pattern Candidates
- Deviations
- Confidence
- Unknowns
